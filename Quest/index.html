HUNT FOR GREAT WEAPON

T'HO'TEP AND ASSOCIATES MAIN QUEST CURRENTLY IS TO FIND GREAT WEAPON FOR T'HO'TEP. NOT THAT T'HO'TEP NEED ONE. T'HO'TEP UNDEFEATABLE ALREADY BUT BETTER WEAPON IS STILL BETTER. T'HO'TEP HAVE MANY SIDE QUESTS THAT INTERLEAVE WITH MAIN QUEST REGARDING GREAT WEAPON.
